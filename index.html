Ensure Your Medicines Are Safe and Authentic

With Med-e-cine, easily verify the authenticity of your medicines by scanning the QR code on the packaging. Our platform also allows you to report any suspicious activity by manufacturers or retailers.

Important Precautions Before Consuming Medicines

  • Always check the expiry date on the packaging.
  • Consult your healthcare provider before starting any new medication.
  • Ensure that the packaging is intact and has not been tampered with.
  • Verify the medicine’s authenticity using our QR code scanning feature.
  • Be aware of the correct dosage and administration instructions.
  • Store medicines according to the instructions on the label.
  • Keep medicines out of reach of children and pets.
  • Dispose of expired or unused medicines safely as per guidelines.
